PayPal Issues

My PayPal payment is not working? If your PayPal payment is not working it could be due to a variety of reasons. If you have clicked "Checkout with PayPal" and nothing happens or you only see a blank window, then you most likely have a pop-up blocker installed. This pop-up blocker will block PayPal from opening an additional payment window. So all you need to do is temporarily disable it and then the window can open or load properly.
