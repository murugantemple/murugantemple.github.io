Murugan Temple Volunteers

Murugan Temple is run by volunteers like you. You can join our global community of volunteers by simply scanning the barcode above or by visiting the link here. You will be redirected to our WhatsApp group where you can join and begin participating. It's simple as 1-2-3.

Our volunteer activities includes:

  • General
  • Administration
  • Cultural committee
  • Fund Raising Activities
  • Finance committee
  • Front-Desk management
  • Garland and Puja
  • Food and Kitchen
  • Newsletter committee
  • Library (Thiru Muruga Kirupanandha Variyar Swamy Library)
  • Religious activities and preparation
  • Temple Beautification
  • Website Support
  • Youth Activities and Education
  • Infrastructure Maintenance
  • Gardening and Outside Beautification
  • Public Relations committee
  • Fund Raising Activities
  • Sound, Music System, Stage Lighting Engineer
  • Networking Engineer
  • Telephony Support
  • Square Register Support
Not finding an activity of interest in the list above, then simple come by the temple on a weekend and helps us in whatever capacity you can. You will find that we are a very friendly group of volunteers working hard each day to help build this temple and strengthen it's legacy for all future generations.

You can contact our current "Volunteer Committee" chair, Ms. Mythili Arun at mythiliarun7@gmail.com or one of our current chairs for the various committees that we have at Murugan Temple. The list of current committees and chair can be found here.

We look forward to your support and participation! This will be a great way to meet new friends in your community with similar interests while helping Lord Muruga, hinduism and our traditions.

If you are already volunteering and serving Lord Muruga, then we thank you from our bottom of our heart for your service and dedication! You've made a difference at Murugan Temple!

May Lord Muruga Shower You And Your Family With His Blessings!
